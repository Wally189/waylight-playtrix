Recommended Fields

What each contact record should hold

Name and organisation
Primary contact, business name, role, and relationship type.
Practical contact details
Email, phone, website, postal notes, and preferred communication route.
Operational context
Current project, contract status, important dates, and what is waiting on whom.
Linked documents
Questionnaires, agreements, proposals, notes, and any relevant SOP or policy.
