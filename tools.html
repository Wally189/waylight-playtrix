iTool discipline

Use the deck as a quiet launcher, not as a place to browse aimlessly. Click a tool to open its saved link or set one if none exists yet. Drag links onto tool pills to store them quickly. Drag tool pills to reorder them. Drag whole lines by their handle to reorder the deck.
