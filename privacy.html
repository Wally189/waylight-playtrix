Data Protection

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data this website may process, why it is processed, and what rights you have.

Waylight Atlantic keeps data use proportionate to a small business website and avoids unnecessary tracking.

Controller

Who operates this website

Data controller: Waylight Atlantic

Location: Bristol, United Kingdom

Privacy enquiries: Use the Contact page. A dedicated privacy email address is not currently published on this website.

Data Collected

What personal data may be collected

Category What this includes
Contact form data Name, email address, and message content submitted through the Contact form.
Technical request data Standard technical request information created by website infrastructure (for example IP address, browser type, and timestamps).
Functional browser storage On the Pricing page only, a currency preference and exchange-rate cache stored in local browser storage.

Purpose and Basis

How data is used and lawful basis

Purpose Lawful basis
Responding to enquiries Legitimate interests and, where relevant, steps at your request before entering a service arrangement.
Maintaining service records Legitimate interests in running and protecting a small business service.
Operating essential site functions Legitimate interests in stable website operation (including currency display preference on the Pricing page).

Third-Party Services

Services actually used on this site

Contact form processing: Formspree is used to deliver contact form submissions.

Font delivery: Google Fonts is loaded from fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com.

Optional pricing conversion: If you select Ireland pricing, the Pricing page requests an exchange rate from the Frankfurter API.

Not currently used: No analytics platform, tracking pixel, newsletter tool, embedded map, or embedded video is loaded by default.

Cookies and Similar Tech

Cookie use on this website

This website does not currently set analytics or advertising cookies.

No cookie banner is currently shown because non-essential tracking cookies are not in use.

The site does use limited local browser storage on the Pricing page to remember currency selection and cache exchange-rate data for a short period.

Cookie and storage controls remain available in your browser settings.

Retention, Rights, and Complaints

How long data is kept and your rights

Enquiry data is kept only for as long as needed to reply, manage service follow-up, and keep basic business records.

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or data portability where applicable.

To exercise rights, use the Contact page and mark your request as privacy-related.

If you are in the UK, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). If you are in Ireland, you can complain to the Data Protection Commission (DPC).

Security and Updates

Security approach and policy updates

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to reduce unauthorised access, misuse, and accidental loss.

No system can be guaranteed as completely risk-free, but Waylight Atlantic aims to keep exposure low through simple, maintainable architecture.

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version published on this page is the one in force.

Related page: See GDPR for rights-focused reference.
